Lasagna Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Ground Beef or Sausage
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Tomato Paste
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Egg
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Italian Herbs (Basil, Oregano)

Steps to Make Lasagna

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until soft.
  4. Add ground beef or sausage to the skillet and cook until browned. Drain excess fat.
  5. Stir in tomato sauce, tomato paste, salt, pepper, and Italian herbs. Simmer for 15 minutes.
  6. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese with an egg and a pinch of salt until smooth.
  7. In a baking dish, spread a layer of meat sauce on the bottom.
  8. Place a layer of lasagna noodles over the sauce.
  9. Spread a layer of ricotta mixture over the noodles, followed by a layer of mozzarella cheese.
  10. Repeat layers, ending with a layer of noodles and meat sauce on top.
  11. Sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese over the top layer.
  12. Cover the dish with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  13. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until the top is golden and bubbly.
  14. Let the lasagna cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
