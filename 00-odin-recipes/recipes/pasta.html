Pasta Ingredients

  • Pasta (e.g., spaghetti, penne)
  • Olive Oil
  • Garlic
  • Tomatoes or Tomato Sauce
  • Basil
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Optional: Red Pepper Flakes

Steps to Make Pasta

  1. Boil a pot of water and add a pinch of salt.
  2. Add the pasta to the boiling water and cook according to package instructions until al dente.
  3. While the pasta is cooking, heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  4. Add minced garlic to the pan and saut√© until fragrant.
  5. Add tomatoes or tomato sauce to the pan and simmer for a few minutes.
  6. Season the sauce with salt, pepper, and optional red pepper flakes.
  7. Drain the pasta and add it to the sauce, tossing to combine.
  8. Sprinkle fresh basil and grated Parmesan cheese over the pasta.
  9. Serve immediately and enjoy your homemade pasta!
