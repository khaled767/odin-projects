Sushi Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Seaweed (Nori)
  • Raw Fish (Salmon, Tuna)
  • Soy Sauce
  • Wasabi
  • Pickled Ginger
  • Cucumber
  • Avocado
  • Sushi Vinegar

Steps to Make Sushi

  1. Rinse the sushi rice until the water runs clear.
  2. Cook the rice in a rice cooker or on the stovetop.
  3. Mix the cooked rice with sushi vinegar and let it cool.
  4. Place a sheet of nori on a bamboo sushi mat.
  5. Spread a thin layer of rice over the nori, leaving a border at the top.
  6. Arrange slices of raw fish, cucumber, and avocado over the rice.
  7. Roll the sushi tightly using the bamboo mat.
  8. Slice the roll into bite-sized pieces with a sharp knife.
  9. Serve with soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger.
